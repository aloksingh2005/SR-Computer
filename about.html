Our Story

How SR Computer became Bihar's most trusted technology solutions provider

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leader

Founded in 2005 by a group of passionate technology enthusiasts, SR Computer started as a small repair shop in Patna with a vision to make quality technology accessible to everyone in Bihar.

Over the past two decades, we've grown from a local repair shop to a comprehensive technology solutions provider, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Bihar. Our journey has been driven by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Today, SR Computer is recognized as one of Bihar's leading technology companies, offering a full range of services from CCTV installation to laptop repairs, printer services, and everything in between.

Company Headquarters - Patna, Bihar
